User Guide

1. Setup & Smart Memory

The app automatically pre-fills your name to save time. Tap the venue box to pick from your previously saved marks (zero typing required). If you choose a saved mark, the app instantly links the correct tide station and fetches the live weather. To log a brand new mark, select "+ Add New Venue", type it in, hit Auto-Search for GPS tides, and the app will remember it forever.

2. Live Logging & Gestures

The Species and Bait lists automatically learn your habits, pinning your Top 5 most frequent catches and Bait Cocktails to the top of the list for 1-tap logging. If the fish are feeding fast, use the "Copy" button to instantly duplicate your last catch. Made a mistake? Just swipe the row to the left to delete it.

3. The xC Rating (Expected Catch)

Think of this exactly like 'xG' in football, but for fishing. xC calculates your performance based on the specific conditions. It is a strictly personal rating that compares today's catch against your own historical average at this exact venue. The algorithm evaluates your baseline catch rate, applies a difficulty multiplier for heavy winds/swells, and heavily rewards you for landing low-probability specimen fish.

4. Analytics Dashboard

At the end of your session, the insight engine crunches the data to reveal your Top Bait, your Peak Feeding window, your overall action pace, and the exact stage of the tide that produced the most bites. All of this is visualized on a custom catch timeline graph.
