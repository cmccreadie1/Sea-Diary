User Guide

1. Setup & Smart Memory

The app automatically pre-fills your name. Tap the venue box to pick from previously saved marks, instantly linking the correct tide station. To log a brand new mark, select "+ Add New Venue", type it in, and hit Fetch Data. The app will automatically GPS locate the nearest tide station and remember it forever.

2. Live Logging & Gestures

The Species list automatically pins your Top 5 frequent catches. The "Rapid Entry Card" automatically jumps to your numeric keypad to save screen taps. Made a mistake? Just swipe the row to the left to delete it.

3. Multiplayer Match

Tap 'Live Match' on the Setup screen. Type a PIN (e.g. 1234) to create a room. Tell your mates to type the same PIN, and you will all instantly link together on a live leaderboard.

4. Analytics Dashboard

At the end of your session, the insight engine crunches the data to reveal your Peak Feeding window, your overall action pace, and the exact stage of the tide that produced the most bites.
